# Spec: Ticket Matrix and Potential Payout E2E

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tickets vary by size

The suite SHALL include short, medium and long tickets.

### Requirement: Tickets vary by game, selection and stake

Selections and stakes SHALL differ, and long tickets SHOULD cover all enabled V1 games.

### Requirement: Potential payout is asserted from snapshots

Successful sales SHALL assert line snapshots, stake total, money breakdown and potential payout.
