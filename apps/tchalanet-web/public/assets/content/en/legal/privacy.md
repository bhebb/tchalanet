> **Info:** This page explains **what data** we process, **why**, and **your rights**.

## 1) Data we process
- Account: name, email, locale, preferences.  
- Security logs (access, device, anti‑fraud).  
- Payments: via compliant providers.

## 2) Purposes
- Provide the service (auth, content).  
- Security & fraud prevention.  
- Product improvement (aggregate metrics).

## 3) Sharing & retention
- Limited sharing with necessary providers (payment, analytics).  
- Retention aligned with legal obligations.

## 4) Cookies & analytics
- Session (auth), preferences (language, theme).  
- Aggregate audience measurement.  
- You can adjust settings in your browser.

## 5) Your rights
- Access, rectification, deletion (per law).  
- Opt‑out from marketing.  
- Contact: [/support](/support) or [/contact](/contact).
