> **Info:** This page summarizes common compliance principles to guide operators and players. Always refer to **official notices** for detailed obligations and dates.

## 1) Roles & scope
- **Lottery authority:** publishes notices, governs POS and online interconnection.  
- **Licensed operators:** deploy compliant points of sale and apply official payout tables.  
- **Players:** get clear information (terms, tables, ticket verification).

## 2) POS & interconnection
- Use **approved POS** devices, **connected** to the central platform.  
- Online: platforms must meet technical and control requirements.  
- Transactions close to result time may face special rules.

## 3) Display & information
- Payout tables, terms, and claim procedures accessible.  
- Public ticket verification via **/ticket/:code**.  
- **Responsible gaming** messaging visible.

## 4) Operator checklist
- Licensing up‑to‑date  
- Authorized, connected POS  
- Online platform interconnected  
- Published payout tables & compliant payouts  
- Customer support & dispute handling

## 5) Player checklist
- Read terms & tables  
- Keep your ticket  
- Verify after the draw  
- Ask for help via [/support](/support)
