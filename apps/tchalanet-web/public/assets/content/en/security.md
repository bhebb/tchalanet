> **Attention:** Beware of **fake websites** and **counterfeit tickets**. Always check the URL and keep your ticket.

## 1) Account security
- Enable **two‑factor authentication (2FA)**.  
- Use a **password manager**.  
- Log out on shared devices.

## 2) Tickets & verification
- Ask for a **ticket** with a **QR** and a **public code**.  
- Verify only via **/ticket/:code** or **/verify**.  
- Don’t share your code before results are published.

## 3) Payments & networks
- Prefer private networks (avoid public Wi‑Fi).  
- Check **HTTPS** (lock).  
- Confirm beneficiaries before approving payments.

## 4) Phishing & scams
- Don’t open unexpected attachments.  
- Beware of “guaranteed win” promises.  
- When in doubt, contact [/support](/support).

## 5) Good habits
- Keep your device updated.  
- Set a budget and stick to it.  
- Take regular breaks.
