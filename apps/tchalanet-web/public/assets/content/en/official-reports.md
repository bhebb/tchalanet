> **Info:** This page points you to **official recaps** published by authorities and licensed operators.

## 1) Where to consult
- Official portals, notices, and recap tables (LEH / operators).  
- Media or apps that relay results—prefer the **official source**.

### Common draws (overview)

> **Attention:** If sources disagree, the **latest official notice** prevails (corrections may occur).

## 2) Check a ticket
Use **/ticket/:code** to view public status: **valid**, **pending** (sync), or **void**.  
You can also use **/verify** if you don’t know the direct URL.

## 3) Understanding schedules
- Draw times may change (holidays, local calendars, regulatory decisions).  
- Always rely on the **most recent** official publication.

## 4) After the draw
- Winning tickets follow a **payout process** (amounts, timelines, checks).  
- Keep your ticket until payment is complete; follow operator instructions.
