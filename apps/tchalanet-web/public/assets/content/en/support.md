> **Info:** For urgent questions, attach a **screenshot** and, if relevant, the **ticket code**.

## 1) You’re a player
- Check a ticket: **/ticket/:code** or **/verify**.  
- Safety & prevention: [/security](/security) • [/legal/responsible](/legal/responsible).

## 2) You’re an operator
- Compliance & integration: see [/legal/regulation](/legal/regulation).  
- Technical assistance: include **environment**, **steps**, **errors**.

## 3) When to open a help ticket
- Payment or validation issues.  
- Suspicion of fraud or counterfeit ticket.  
- Result display malfunction.
