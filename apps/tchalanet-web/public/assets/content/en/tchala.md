> **Info:** *Tchala* is part of popular culture. It offers **dream–number** associations passed down orally. It is **not predictive**.

## 1) Where does Tchala come from?
Historically, people related certain **dreams** to **numbers** to remember them or “try their luck.” Lists vary by neighborhood and family.

## 2) Illustrative examples
<div class="md-scroll">

| Dream (ex.) | Suggested number |
|---|---:|
| Wedding | 17 |
| Market | 23 |
| Rain | 07 |
| Snake | 33 |
| Baby | 05 |
</div>

> **Attention:** Associations change across local traditions. Don’t confuse **symbolism** with **prediction**.

## 3) Playing with discernment
- Treat Tchala as **cultural** and **playful**.  
- Stake only what you can afford to lose.  
- Complement with **security tips** and **responsible gaming**.
