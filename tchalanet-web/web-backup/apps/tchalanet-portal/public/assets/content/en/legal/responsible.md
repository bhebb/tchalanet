> **Info:** Gambling should remain **entertainment**. If you sense a loss of control, talk about it and seek help.

## 1) Set limits
- Weekly or monthly budget, not to be exceeded.  
- Limited play time, planned breaks.  
- Don’t chase losses.

## 2) Warning signs
- Borrowing to gamble, lying to loved ones, neglecting responsibilities.  
- Intrusive thoughts, mood depending on outcomes.  
- Isolation or irritability linked to play.

## 3) Tools
- Self‑exclusion (where available).  
- In‑app reminders/limits.  
- Self‑assessment tools (e.g., Evalujeu).

## 4) Helping someone
- Talk without judgment; suggest support.  
- Point to professionals.  
- In emergencies, contact specialized services.
