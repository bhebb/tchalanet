> **Info:** This page explains common formats (borlette, 3–5D lotto, marriage) and the end‑to‑end flow. To check a ticket: **/ticket/:code** or **/verify**.

## 1) Game landscape
**Borlette (2 digits)** and **lotto 3/4/5** are number games rooted in Haitian culture. **Marriage** (two numbers combined, e.g., 12×34) has its own payout tables. You pick numbers, place a stake, get a ticket, then compare with **official results** after the draw.

### Key formats (at a glance)
<div class="md-scroll">

| Format | Goal | Number range | Example | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Borlette (2D) | 2 exact digits | 00–99 | 27 | Flexible stake, operator payout table |
| Lotto 3D | 3 exact digits | 000–999 | 123 | Higher odds than 2D |
| Lotto 4D | 4 exact digits | 0000–9999 | 0123 | Harder, higher payout potential |
| Lotto 5D | 5 exact digits | 00000–99999 | 54321 | Long odds, highest payout potential |
| Marriage | Two numbers combined | — | 12×34 | Specific tables by operator |
</div>

> **Attention:** Payout tables vary by operator and current regulation. Always check the information in‑app or at the point of sale.

## 2) Commonly followed draws
Players often watch **National**, **New York**, and **Miami** draws. Schedules and official recaps can evolve; always trust the **latest official publication**. See [/official-reports](/official-reports).

## 3) From stake to verification: the simple flow
1. Pick the **format** (2D, 3D, 4D, 5D, marriage).  
2. Enter your **number(s)** and **stake**.  
3. Get a **ticket** with **QR** and a **signed public code**.  
4. After the draw, check **/ticket/:code** (valid / pending / void).

### Helpful tips
- Set a **budget** and stick to it.  
- Avoid last‑minute online stakes near result time—special rules may apply.  
- Keep your ticket (or digital copy) until any claim is settled.

## 4) Example (learning scenario)
You stake **20 G** on **27** in 2D. Your ticket is emitted with QR + code. After the draw, consult the official recap; if **27** is drawn, your payout is calculated per the current table.
